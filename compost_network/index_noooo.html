    Compost Waste Network(CWN)


    About C.W. Network

    Composting, is a biochemical process by which Organic Waste Materials(Refuse) are decomposed under certain conditions by action of Microbes(Fungi, Bacteria etc.) acting on it which reduces the volume of the waste product by at least 60%. Composite Materials can range from Eggshells, Fruits, Vegetable, Paper, Diary, Yard waste, Houseplants, Nut Shells etc. The end product of this catalyzed Bio-chemical reaction is called Humus or Compost.

    We designed our application (Compost Waste Network) to allow people to continue compositing in a best and most comfortable way while reducing Climatic Changes caused by Methane emissions.
    The application was designed to allow People and various Local communities with backyard composts bins to collect and share composts to and from neighbors,one another etc. This is aimed and geared towards allowing the re-use of compost materials within People and their various Local communities.

    Powered For Smart People & Communities.


    How it Works/What It Does

    1.)It allow Users to add a Compost Drop-Off Location Address for People and various Communities to bring their Compost to.

    2.)It allow People to look for nearby Drop-Off Locations to bring their Yard Waste,Houseplants, Food Scraps, Vegetable, Paper, Diary etc. to

    3.)It allow People to View The Compost Nearby Drop-Off Locations from the Post Sections and on the Google Maps

    4.)It allows Community Members to Interacts and connects to one another via Posts and Comments section for each published Compost Drop-off Locations.

    5.) It allows each community Members to get Notification alerts each time a new Compost Drop-Off Location is added/published as well as each time someone reacts in the Comments Sections for each updated Compositing locations. This ensures that you will not miss any updates in real-time. You can comment/give review about the Compost drop-off locations in a comment section for each compositing locations.
